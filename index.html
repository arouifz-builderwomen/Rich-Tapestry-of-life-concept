THE PLACE

Where Time Slows And Life Expands

Perched above the Akamas Peninsula, Drouseia exists in that rare space between mountain and sea, between memory and possibility.

Here, mornings begin quietly. Lunch stretches lazily into afternoon. Conversations continue long after sunset. The landscape invites you to slow down, to notice more, and to rediscover the luxury of time.

This is not a place designed to impress. It is a place designed to be lived.

TAPESTRY HOUSE

More Than A House. More Than A Destination.

Tapestry House was inspired by a simple idea.

The best memories are rarely made in grand moments. They are created around long tables, over shared meals, through unexpected conversations and in the company of people who make us feel at home.

Built on the edge of the Akamas Peninsula, Tapestry House is a gathering place for family, friends and future stories.

A place where people arrive as guests and leave as part of the tapestry.

THE SEASONS

A Different Story Every Season

Spring brings wildflowers across the hillsides and walking trails alive with colour.

Summer unfolds through sea breezes, long lunches and evenings beneath endless stars.

Autumn arrives in golden light, harvest celebrations and quieter rhythms.

Winter reveals another side of Cyprus: dramatic skies, fireside conversations and a village returning to itself.

The Most Valuable Destinations Are Not Found On A Map

They are discovered through people, stories and moments that remain with us long after the journey ends.
